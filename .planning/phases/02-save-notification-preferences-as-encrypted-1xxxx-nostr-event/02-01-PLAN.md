<objective>
Establish the pure, unit-testable core of Phase 2: the functions that turn an
AppConfig into the rules-only sync payload and back, validate an untrusted
inbound payload, and provide the two conflict-resolution primitives (newest-wins
high-water-mark and payload-equality) that the reactive service (Plan 03)
composes. Every function here is pure and synchronous, so it is exercised with
`bun:test` without touching RelayPool, EventStore, or a live signer — mirroring
the helpers/groups.ts split that keeps this layer testable (Pitfall 6 / WR-04).

The one "integration-lite" test in this plan builds a real kind-30078 event with
a `PrivateKeySigner`, self-encrypts the payload with NIP-44, signs it, and proves
it decrypts back through applesauce's `app-data` read helpers — validating the
D2-01/D2-02/D2-03 manual publish shape and the interop-read path in one shot,
without importing services/nostr.ts.

Purpose: Everything downstream (Plan 03's publish + subscribe pipelines) depends
on these exact symbols and their JSON contract. Proving them first (Wave 1)
de-risks the whole phase — the serialization, validation, and conflict logic is
verified before any RxJS/relay wiring exists.

Output: `helpers/preferences.ts` with nine exports and a green `bun:test` suite
covering D2-04, D2-05, D2-06, D2-08, D2-09, and the D2-01/D2-02/D2-03 round-trip.

Note on divergence: the ROADMAP phase title says "1xxxx nostr event"; D2-01
supersedes it with NIP-78 kind 30078 (a parameterized-replaceable app-data kind).
PREFS_KIND is defined as 30078 accordingly.
</objective>
